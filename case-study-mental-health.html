Case Study: Mental Health & TMS/Spravato

Optimizing CPT/ICD Coding and Revenue Cycle for Innovative Mental Health Treatments

Challenge

Tidemark Therapy, one of the fastest-growing mental health providers in the USA, was facing significant challenges with their revenue cycle management, particularly around complex CPT and ICD-10 coding for innovative treatments like Spravato (esketamine) and Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS). These cutting-edge treatments required specialized coding knowledge that was difficult to maintain across their expanding network of clinics.

Key challenges included:

  • High denial rates (28%) for Spravato and TMS treatment claims due to coding errors
  • Inconsistent application of appropriate mental health diagnosis codes
  • Difficulty tracking the nuanced documentation requirements for these specialized treatments
  • Significant revenue leakage estimated at $450,000 annually
  • Clinical staff spending 12+ hours per week on administrative coding tasks

Solution

Klyrone implemented a comprehensive AI solution combining our Clinical Diagnosis Assistant and RCM Optimization Suite, specifically tailored for mental health services and specialized treatments.

Specialized Mental Health Coding Engine

We developed a specialized AI model trained on mental health documentation, treatment protocols, and coding guidelines specific to Spravato and TMS treatments. This engine could identify the subtle clinical indicators that justify specific procedure and diagnosis codes.

Documentation Enhancement

Our system analyzed clinical notes in real-time to identify missing elements required for proper coding and reimbursement of specialized treatments, prompting providers to include necessary documentation before claim submission.

Payer-Specific Intelligence

We incorporated payer-specific requirements for mental health treatments, especially for newer therapies like Spravato and TMS, ensuring claims met the exact documentation and coding standards of each insurance provider.

Agentic Claim Review

Our agentic AI system performed autonomous pre-submission claim reviews, identifying potential issues and suggesting corrections based on historical denial patterns and current payer guidelines.

Implementation Approach

1

Initial Assessment

We conducted a comprehensive analysis of Tidemark's existing coding practices, denial patterns, and documentation workflows for Spravato and TMS treatments.

2

Custom Model Training

We trained our AI models using anonymized clinical documentation and claims data specific to mental health treatments, with special focus on Spravato and TMS procedures.

3

Integration

Our solution was integrated with Tidemark's existing EHR and practice management systems, ensuring seamless workflow incorporation without disrupting clinical operations.

4

Pilot Program

We launched a 60-day pilot program at three Tidemark locations, allowing for real-time adjustments and optimization before full-scale deployment.

5

Full Deployment

Following successful pilot results, we rolled out the solution to all Tidemark locations with ongoing monitoring and optimization.
